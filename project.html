PROJECT DETAILS

  • TITLE:

    Digital circular using Arduino and GSM for Schools and Colleges

  • Domain/ Technology used:

    Micro processor and Micro controller.

  • Abstract:

    It presents an SMS based notice board incorporating the widely used GSM to facilitate the communication of displaying message on notice board via user'smobile phone. SIM900 GSM modem with a SIM card is interfaced to the ports of the microcontroller with the help of AT commands.
